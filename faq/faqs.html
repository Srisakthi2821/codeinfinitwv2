FAQ

1What is CodeInfinite?

CodeInfinite is a leading software training institute in Coimbatore, dedicated to providing meticulous training in trending technologies. We empower aspiring candidates to realize their IT dreams through job-oriented training.

2When was CodeInfinite founded?

CodeInfinite officially started its journey on January 19, 2018.

3Is CodeInfinite affiliated with any other organization?

Yes, CodeInfinite is a division of Sangam Soft Solutions, a reputable software development company founded in 2013.

4What courses does CodeInfinite offer?

We offer a wide range of courses, including C, C++, Java, Python, software testing, MySQL, Android development, Django, J2EE, and web development (front-end and fullstack).

5Who are the instructors at CodeInfinite?

Our instructors are experienced professionals with expertise in various fields. You can find detailed instructor profiles on our website.

6How can I enrol in a course at CodeInfinite?

To enrol in a course, please visit our "Courses" page, select your desired course, and follow the registration process. You can also contact us for assistance.

7What is the duration of the courses?

Course durations vary depending on the specific program. You can find course duration information on the individual course pages.

8Is financial aid or scholarships available?

We offer various scholarship programs and financial aid options. Please contact our admissions team for details and eligibility criteria.

9How can I track my course progress and attendance?

You can track your course progress, attendance, and other details through your personalized dashboard on our website.

10What are the office hours of CodeInfinite?

Our office hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM from Monday to Saturday and from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM on Sundays.

11How can I contact CodeInfinite for inquiries or support?

You can reach us by phone at +91 99764 99765 or via email at info@codeinfinite.in. You can also visit our office during our office hours.
